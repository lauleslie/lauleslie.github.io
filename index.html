HomeMe

HomeMe is an application powered by Web2py. You are able to posts your needs in a forum-like area.

If you make a landlord post, you can upload a picture of your property and list details. If you make a tenant post, you can let others know about yourself, your budget, and the number of people you have.

I worked as the product owner and lead front-end developer in a team of 5 people.
